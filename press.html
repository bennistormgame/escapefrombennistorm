Description

Short: A retro arcade brawler with a crime-comedy soul. 100 levels, 34 playable misfits, five neon worlds — one way out of Bennistorm.

Long: It was meant to be one big night out. It became the longest night of their lives. Bennistorm doesn't let people leave — the doors lock, the clock starts, and the only way home is through five neon neighbourhoods crawling with Benni Storm's Outfit. Grab your squad, eat the right sweets, dodge the runners, rescue your mates and fight your way out. Underneath the arcade chaos is a full cinematic story, an arcade-within-the-arcade, and four original albums made for the heist.

Key features

  • 100 hand-built levels across 5 neon worlds
  • 34 playable characters, each with a unique special
  • A full cinematic Episode One story
  • 21 arcade cabinets (1 secret) — a whole arcade hidden inside
  • Four original albums — 43 tracks (synth, rock, spy-jazz)
  • Local co-op — escape together
  • Boss rescues, secret rooms and unlockable crew
  • Built by one director and a crew of AI specialists
